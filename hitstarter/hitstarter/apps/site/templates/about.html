About HitStarter


HitStarter is a hidden space for anonymous crowdfunding. Some projects are just a bit too ambitious for the normal internet, so we're building a place where people can raise funds for creative ideas which need a bit more discretion.

Mission

HitStarter aims to provide a secure and anonymous space for individuals and organizations to raise money from a broad base of contributors in order to fund projects where organizers or contributors require anonymity.
Some examples may include:

  • Activist groups which wish to raise money for materials/propaganda/demonstrations without exposing their contributors.
  • Whistleblowers who wish to raise money to fund their escape plans and/or legal defenses.
  • Scientists who wish to raise money for unorthadox experiments without the fear of being expelled from academia.
  • Women in Islamic countries who wish to raise funds for organizing support groups or planned actions.
  • Homosexuals who wish to raise money for the production for educational or sexual materials in places where such activities are illegal.
  • Freedom fighters who wish to raise money from international sources for their armed insurrections against oppressive regimes.
  • ..and many more!

Governance

HitStarter will not be a completely open platform - it will not be used to raise money to fund the production of child pornography, organized violence against undeserving populations, or any other blantanly repulsive purpose. However, there are many issues which are not as clear cut, and may require deliberation and debate in order to reconcile our freedom-promoting mission with our desire to respect and nurture basic human rights.

In order to maintain the development and operations of the service, as well as to make the executive decisions on which content should be allowed and which shouldn't, an autonymous executive committee will be formed. Those who have the technical, social or moral credentials necessary to be on such a committee should please get in touch.

Open

HitStarter is an open source project. Contributions welcome!
